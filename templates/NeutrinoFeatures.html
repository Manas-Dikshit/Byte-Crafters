Neutrino AI - Features

Conversational AI Chat Support

See How

Neutrino provides empathetic, real-time mental health support through AI-powered conversations. It listens, understands emotions, and offers guidance using therapy-based techniques. Users can talk about stress, anxiety, or general well-being, and the bot responds with calming strategies, affirmations, and actionable advice tailored to their needs for emotional relief.

Example: A user feeling anxious starts chatting with the bot.

Sentiment Analysis

See How

Neutrino tracks emotional trends over time, analyzing daily mood inputs and text interactions. By identifying stress patterns and emotional triggers, it helps users understand their mental health better. It offers insights, suggests preventive strategies, and provides self-care recommendations to improve long-term emotional resilience and overall psychological well-being.

Example: User logs emotions daily, and AI detects mood patterns.

Personalized Diet Plans

See How

Neutrino crafts customized meal plans based on dietary needs, health goals, and food preferences. Whether a user wants to lose weight, gain muscle, or manage a health condition, the AI curates science-backed diet suggestions. It adapts recommendations dynamically, ensuring balanced, nutritious, and goal-oriented meal planning.

Example: User inputs dietary restrictions, and AI generates a meal plan.

Nutritional Analysis Tool

See How

Neutrino analyzes meals, breaking them down into macronutrients, micronutrients, and caloric intake. Users can scan, input, or describe food items, and the bot provides insights on how their diet aligns with health goals. It also suggests healthier alternatives and tracks daily nutritional balance for optimal well-being.

Example: User scans a food item, and the bot breaks down its nutrients.
